# Research Plan

## User Goal
Compare semi-supervised brain tumor segmentation methods and identify research gaps.

## Research Mode
Combination of Literature Review Mode and Research Advisor Mode.

## Scope
- Domain: Medical AI, semi-supervised brain tumor segmentation in MRI.
- Time Range: Focus on recent works since 2019.
- Datasets: BraTS dataset primarily.
- Methods: Semi-supervised learning techniques, including teacher-student, pseudo-labeling, cross-modality translation, uncertainty-aware models.
- Evaluation: Metrics like Dice score, Hausdorff distance.
- Deliverables: Literature review, evidence matrix, research gaps, feasible research ideas.

## Query Strategy
- Core Query: "semi-supervised brain tumor segmentation"
- Query Variants:
  - "label efficient brain tumor segmentation"
  - "limited annotation medical image segmentation"
  - "BraTS semi supervised segmentation"
  - "pseudo label consistency regularization medical image segmentation"
  - "domain shift external validation brain tumor segmentation"

## Source Strategy
- arXiv for recent AI/ML methods.
- PubMed for biomedical imaging and clinical relevance.
- Web search if needed for dataset or code pages.

## Expected Outputs
- Structured literature comparison table.
- Identification of research gaps across methods.
- Suggestions for research directions based on evidence.

## Risks
- Limited real-world clinical validation in existing literature.
- Variability in datasets and metrics complicating direct comparisons.
